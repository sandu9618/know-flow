## 📝 How to Write Each Article

**Structure that performs well on Medium:**

1. **Hook** — a relatable problem or surprising fact (2–3 lines)
2. **What you'll learn** — set expectations
3. **The concept** — explain it simply
4. **The code** — real, working snippets
5. **What went wrong** — honest mistakes build trust
6. **Key takeaways** — 3 bullet points max

**Target:** 800–1,500 words. Not too short, not overwhelming.
